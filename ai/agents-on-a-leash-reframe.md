Note:
So if non-determinism is the problem, here's the good news: we solved it a long time ago. We just didn't solve it for robots - we solved it for you. We don't let an engineer SSH straight into production at 3 in the morning and run whatever they want. You open a pull request. Someone reviews it. A policy checks it. A pipeline applies it. We built an entire discipline around the fact that people are fallible and non-deterministic. An AI agent isn't a new kind of threat - it's the same kind of actor we already learned to handle. It's just knocking on a door we locked years ago.
